Fitcoin - Fun Fitness for All!

Our mission statment is to provide a fun alternative for those who feel alienated and intimidated by fitness. Helping adults who are short on time and motivation to reach their long-term health goals through social gaming.

Needfinding

Through conducting several user interviews, we developed empathy maps and insights to our problem space of personalized health tracking

POV, HMWs and Experience Prototypes

Additional needfinding data allowed us to create revised points of views. These enabled us to create potential solutions; understand the solutions' assumptions and test those using experience prototypes

Concept Video

Using our needfinding and solution ideas, we came up with a concept video

Low-Fi Prototype

Creating several sketches of different forms of our solution allowed us to decide on creating a mobile app. Using invision, we created our first prototype

Med-Fi Prototype

Building upon revisions and feedback on our low-fi prototype, we created a med-fi prototype in Sketch

Heurisitic Evaluation

Following our Med-fi prototype, a group of our classmates provided us with a detailed evaluation on Fitcoin's design
